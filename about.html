Our Story

As MBA students at the University of Kerala, AmalRaj, Rahul, and Tinu witnessed firsthand the challenges students face in connecting with quality recruiters. Frustrated by the outdated campus placement systems, they combined their skills in technology, business strategy, and operations to create Kampus Connect - a platform that bridges the gap between talented students and forward-thinking companies.

What started as a classroom project in 2023 has now transformed into Kerala's fastest-growing campus recruitment platform, serving over 20 colleges and 100+ recruiters across India.
